About

The concept of Manila kitchen network is to connect Local food businesses, and focus on pure Filipino food business Under the Metro Manila premises. In Manila kitchen network customers Will be able to see food businesses that are blooming and that are new. Food businesses can list their business with Manila kitchen network Including eateries and local canteens. As Manila kitchen network is fund Of Philippine food history we also give attention to classical and Traditional foods to preserve our colorful food history.
